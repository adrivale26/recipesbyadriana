Soft and Warm Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

As the holiday season gets closer, the cravings for sweet treats get higher. Chocolate chip cookies are delicious, soft, and simple to make using these ingredients:

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 cup of butter
  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 3/4 cup of brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • Chunky chocolate chips
  • For extra flavor, 1 teaspoon of cinnamon

Mix all dry ingredients into a large bowl and mix. Next mix all the wet ingredients into a separate smaller bowl. Lastly, combine all ingredients into a large bowl and mix well.

Add as many chocolate chips as you like and for extra flavor add cinnamon. Bake for about 8-12 minutes at 350 degrees.
